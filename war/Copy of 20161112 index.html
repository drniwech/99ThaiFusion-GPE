About 99 Thai Fusion

Pronounce: ninety-nine Thai Fusion. What is ๙๙ ? It is number 99 in Thai language. Number 9 is the lucky number in which believed that it will bring a good fortune in Thai culture. The Thai word for nine, Gao, sounds similar to the following Thai words; first one is Gao-Knah, can be translated to progress or moving forward. Second, Thai rice is called, Khaw. So we named our restaurant as 99 Thai Fusion to represent those meanings. We serve authentic Thai rice and meals as our chefs select ingredients and cook in Thai authentic ways. We decorate our restaurant with Thai crafts and furniture’s so that you feel like dining in Thailand. We would like to ensure that you and your love ones have the best experience and moments to celebrate your everyday and special days at 99 Thai Fusion.
